Hi, I’m Wen-Yu Chen, a photographer. 19 years old, like to captures life through the lens. I’m drawn to the quiet moments of light and shadow that slip into everyday life — the ones that gently move the heart. To me, photography is more than documentation; it’s a way of storytelling. Every image is a quiet narrative in itself. If my work resonates with you, look to collaborations — feel free to contact me.
